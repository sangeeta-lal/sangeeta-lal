    Short Biography

    Sangeeta is a lecturer in Data Science at Keele University, United Kingdom. Her primary research can be broadly classified into Software Engineering, Data Science, and Machine Learning. She joined Keele University in September 2020 holding the role of program director.

    She has previously worked as an assistant professor at the MACS department at Heriot-Watt University and the Department of Computer Science at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology.
